No Wrong Door…and Many Right Doors

MBS Behavioral Health & Wellness has a “No Wrong Door” policy. This means that you will be assessed and receive care and treatment no matter where you enter our system. This includes service supports and collaboration to help address your identified needs. If there is a problem with eligibility for services, you will be assisted in finding the right supports and services in the community as well. We strive to provide seamless and personally meaningful care.

Our Statment

At MBS Behavioral Health & Wellness, LLC, we believe that ALL healing begins in the mind. We provide effective, comprehensive, quality therapy and treatment to help you cope with your struggles whether related to mental illness, developmental disability, marital/family/relationship discord, bariatric surgery, or substance use disorder.

Our Guiding Principles

Your care is comprehensive. Your care is timely You and your recovery are priorities Your care environment is of great importance Your care is targeted to your situation. Your care is delivered in an open, accepting environment. Your feedback is essential.
